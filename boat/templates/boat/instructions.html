Instructions

To win the game you need to navigate the boat safely to land (green square); If you make contact with one of the enemy ships you will lose.

Controls

To start the game press the 'Start Game' button. Say 'north', 'south', 'west', or 'east' into you microphone to change the boats direction. Say 'halt' to stop the boat. {% endblock %}
